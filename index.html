In Greek mythology, Chiron was the great teacher and mentor of heroes — among them Achilles, Jason, and Asclepius. Unlike other centaurs, who were known for wildness and brute force, Chiron was distinguished by wisdom, discipline, and an extraordinary ability to cultivate greatness in those he guided. Philosophy

Honest counsel is the only kind worth paying for

The admissions consulting industry has a documented problem with telling clients what they want to hear. Applications are polished, narratives are inflated, and expectations are managed — until the decisions arrive.

I operate differently. My job is not to make you feel good about your application. My job is to give you the most accurate, strategic picture of your candidacy and then help you execute as well as the profile supports.

That means telling some prospective clients that I am not the right fit for them — that their LSAT score or GPA places T14 admissions outside the realistic range and that paying me $7,000 would not change that. Those are uncomfortable conversations. I have them anyway.

The applicants I do take on deserve a consultant who has maintained that standard.
