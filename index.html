Chiron

KY-ron

In Greek mythology, Chiron was the wisest of the centaurs — a teacher of heroes. Unlike the other centaurs, he was known not for wildness but for knowledge, medicine, and the patient instruction of those who would go on to do great things.

The name was chosen deliberately. This practice is built on the same premise: rigorous preparation, honest counsel, and the belief that the right guidance changes outcomes.
